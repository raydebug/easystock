# Comprehensive Fundamental Report for TSM (Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company Limited)

## Company Profile
**Name:** Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company Limited  
**Sector:** Technology  
**Industry:** Semiconductors  
**Market Cap:** $1.92 Trillion (USD)  
**Current Date:** 2026-04-11  

TSMC is the world's largest dedicated semiconductor foundry and a critical player in the global technology supply chain. The company manufactures advanced logic chips and advanced semiconductor devices, supplying major technology companies worldwide. TSMC plays a central role in the tech ecosystem, producing chips for various industries from smartphones and personal computers to advanced AI accelerators and automotive systems.

4. **Competitive Pressure:** Rising competition from other foundries could impact pricing and market share.
