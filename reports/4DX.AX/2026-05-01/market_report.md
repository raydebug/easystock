# 4DX.AX Technical Analysis Report

## Executive Summary

4DX.AX has demonstrated extreme volatility following a parabolic rally in late March 2026, correcting significantly from nearly $8.00 to around $4.01 over recent weeks. While the overall trend remains bearish, this latest correction presents an interesting technical recovery scenario with mixed signals.

## Key Technical Observations

### Price Action & Moving Averages
- **Long-term Trend**: Bullish since November 2025, with price at $4.01 (April 30) significantly above the 200 SMA of $2.77
- **Medium-term**: Current price slightly below 50 SMA ($4.88), indicating some medium-term selling pressure
- **Short-term**: Recent recovery attempt moving price back above both 10 SMA and 50 SMA by April 30

### MACD Analysis
- **Momentum Shift**: MACD crossed above the signal line on April 30, transitioning from negative to positive territory
- **Signal Quality**: This crossover came as the MACD bottomed out, providing a potential reversal signal
- **Historical Context**: The recent $0.275 negative histogram reading signals waning momentum, but potential for recovery

### RSI Indicator
- **Extreme Levels**: RSI surged to over 71 (overbought territory) during April 10-13
- **Current Status**: RSI is now around 33.8-35.9, trading significantly below the 50 midpoint
- **Support Indication**: Being near the 30 level typically provides technical support floor

### Volatility Analysis
- **ATR Reading**: Average true range has stabilized around 0.49-0.54, showing reduced volatility from recent extreme levels
- **Trading Range**: Current consolidation suggests traders are waiting for fresh signals

## Trading Implications

### Bullish Case
1. **Crossover Confirmation**: Recent MACD and price action crossovers suggest momentum shift
2. **Moving Average Squeeze**: Both SMA and EMA crossovers indicate potential short-term recovery
3. **RSI Floor**: Trading near 30-40 provides technical support
4. **Historical Trend**: Long-term bullish structure remains intact above 200 SMA

### Bearish Risks
1. **Trend Continuation**: The broader downtrend from $6.00+ remains intact
2. **Volume Uncertainty**: Low volume during recent recovery leaves questions about sustainability
3. **Psychological Levels**: Traders may resist buying near previous support zones

## Recommended Strategy

### Short-term Trading Approach
**Entry**: Consider long positions if price holds above $4.00 with confirmation
**Stop Loss**: Below $3.80 (recent support level)
**Target**: $4.60 - $5.00 (resistance near April levels)

### Position Sizing
**Maximum Risk**: Not recommended to exceed 3-5% of portfolio
**Timeframe**: Best positioned for intraday to 3-day holding period

## Risk Management Assessment
- Volatility remains elevated despite ATR stabilizing
- Rapid price changes suggest maintaining tight stop losses
- Be prepared for continued range-bound trading behavior

## Conclusion

4DX.AX shows potential for short-term recovery as price attempts to stabilize around current support levels. However, the absence of strong volume and the broader downtrend from peak levels suggest caution. Traders should wait for confirmation signals before entering positions and maintain strict risk controls given the stock's history of extreme price swings.
