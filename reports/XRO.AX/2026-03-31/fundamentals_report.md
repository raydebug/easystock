# Comprehensive Fundamental Analysis Report: Xero Limited (XRO.AX)

## Company Overview

**Company Name**: Xero Limited  
**Ticker**: XRO.AX  
**Sector**: Technology - Software Application  
**Market Cap**: AUD 12.24 Billion  
**Industry**: Cloud-based accounting software platform

Xero Limited is a leading cloud-based accounting software company that provides financial management solutions to small and medium-sized businesses. The company has built a substantial international customer base with operations primarily in New Zealand, Australia, and the United Kingdom.
