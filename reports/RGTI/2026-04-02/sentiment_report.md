## Executive Summary

Rigetti Computing (RGTI) has been under intense focus recently following a major strategic announcement to invest up to $100 million in expanding quantum operations in the UK, while simultaneously navigating significant market volatility. Vertical integration at this facility could provide advantages in scaling production and controlling quality.
