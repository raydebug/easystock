## Executive Summary

UnitedHealth Group (UNH) operates as a diversified healthcare company with two primary segments: Optum (health services) and UnitedHealthcare (health insurance). Despite recent challenges in operating income, the company maintains strong cash generation capabilities, industry-leading market position, and a competitive dividend yield. The current market capitalization stands at approximately $279.3 billion, positioning UNH as a major player in the healthcare services sector.

## Company Profile

UnitedHealth Group is focused on improving the health system of those it serves by connecting people with care, simplifying the health care experience, and promoting wellness. The company operates through two main segments:

1. **UnitedHealthcare:** Provides health care services primarily through managed care and other related services
2. **Optum:** Offers services including pharmacy services, provider services, health care technology, and health care services
