## Conclusion Summary

All technical indicators remain bearish for DMC Limited. The stock is in a downtrend with momentum weakening but persisting. The RSI near the lower boundary suggests potential for a counter-trend bounce, but buyers will need to act decisively to push price above the middle band (~17.46). Until there is clear evidence of bullish momentum, maintaining a cautious stance with downside risk management is advised. For swing traders seeking contrarian opportunities, monitoring RSI stabilization and price behavior following a rejection of the lower band could yield potential low-risk setups if support holds.

**Recommended Position:** Wait for clear bullish reversal confirmation before entering long positions. For existing short positions, consider tightening stop losses as price approaches support.
