**Extreme Discrepancy Alert:**
The company shows a concerning price-to-earnings ratio of 94.83x (TTM), which is exceptionally high for an energy company with a 0.26% net profit margin. However, the forward P/E of just 14.30x suggests:

1. **Significant Earnings Growth Expected:** The market is pricing in a potential earnings turnaround

**Price-to-Book Ratio:**
At 2.60x, Ampol trades at a modest premium to book value, indicating reasonable valuation relative to net asset value of approximately $12.74 per share.
