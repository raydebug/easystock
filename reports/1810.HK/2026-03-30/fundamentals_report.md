## 1. COMPANY PROFILE

**Company Name:** Xiaomi Corporation  
**Ticker Symbol:** 1810.HK  
**Sector:** Technology  
**Industry:** Consumer Electronics  
**Exchange:** Hong Kong Stock Exchange  

Xiaomi Corporation is a leading Chinese multinational electronics and IoT company, founded in 2010. It is one of the world's largest smartphone manufacturers, competing with Apple, Samsung, and other major players. Xiaomi's diverse product portfolio includes smartphones, smart home devices, AIoT (Artificial Intelligence of Things) devices, and services. The company has built a strong ecosystem approach, offering an integrated set of products and services centered around connectivity.

### Key Characteristics
- **Market Position:** One of the top global smartphone manufacturers and a leader in the AIoT markets
- **Business Model:** Direct sales, e-commerce, and offline retail channels; diversified product ecosystem
- **Global Reach:** Operates in over 100 countries and regions with localized strategies
- **Brand Positioning:** Positioned as a premium yet accessible technology brand
- **Investment Focus:** Heavy investment in R&D with $24.1 billion spent across 2024-2025
