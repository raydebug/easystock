# SNAP Stock Technical Analysis Report

## Executive Summary

SNAP stock has undergone a dramatic transformation over the past 6 months, experiencing a severe downtrend that recently shows signs of stabilization. The stock has formed a potential double bottom at the $4.00-level and is currently attempting a recovery, but multiple technical indicators suggest the move may be corrective rather than the start of a new sustained uptrend.

## Detailed Technical Analysis

### 1. Price Action & Trend Structure
The visual representation of SNAP's price movement reveals three distinct phases:

**Phase I (October 2025 - November 2025):**
- Strong bullish momentum carried stock from $7.80 to $9.13
- Multiple breakouts and volume spikes marked this period
- Peak at $9.13 on November 12, 2025

**Phase II (December 2025 - March 2026):**
- Seesaw trading pattern between $7.50 and $8.20
- Gradual erosion of support levels
- Breakdown patterns emerging in early 2026

**Phase III (March 2026 - Present):**
- Major downtrend breakdown
- Stock collapsed from $8.10 to a low of $4.01 on March 26, 2026
- Potential double bottom formation at $4.00 and $4.01 levels
- Recovery attempt from $4.01 to current price of $5.15 (April 13, 2026)

## Risk Assessment

**Bearish Risks:**
- Current price below 200 SMA indicating continued long-term downtrend
- Recent recovery lacks volume confirmation
- Resistance at $5.50-$5.80 likely to cap upside initially
- Broader market conditions could support the downtrend continuation

**Bullish Signs:**
- Oversold conditions resolved with confirmed RSI divergence
- Support structure appears to be rebuilding at $4.50 level
- Short-term EMA bullish crossover suggests momentum shift
- Double bottom pattern increases probability of continued recovery

## Trading Recommendations

### For Long-Term Investors
- **HOLD** position - current recovery appears corrective
- Monitor price action at $5.50 first, then $8.20 volume confirmation
- Only consider increasing position size if price breaks and holds above 50 SMA

### For Short-Term Traders
- **WAIT** for breakout confirmation - current price may be forming a triangle pattern
- Consider buying dips toward $4.50-$4.80 for potential rally to $5.50
- Set stops below recent low of $4.01 ($4.00 as critical level)

### Risk Management
- Risk tolerance should be adjusted upward given the recovery volatility
- Position sizing should reflect lower conviction in the trend change
- Monitor for any news events or earnings reports

## Conclusion

SNAP stock is in a critical phase of trend transition. The stock has successfully avoided lower lows and formed a potential double bottom at the $4.00 level. The recovery attempt shows emerging bullish momentum with strong short-term indicators, but the stock remains dramatically below its long-term trend. Until price action confirms sustainability by trading above 50 SMA and then 200 SMA, the trading thesis remains mixed, with a preference toward cautious recovery plays rather than aggressive new positions.

The pattern suggests a likely continuation trade within the $4.50-$5.50 range, with a clear breakout point (above $5.50 with volume) required to validate the sustainability of this recovery attempt.
